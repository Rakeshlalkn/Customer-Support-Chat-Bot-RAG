# Refund Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If something isn't right, here's how refunds work.

## Eligibility
- You can request a refund within **30 days** of purchase for any reason.
- Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and include all accessories.
- Digital products (downloads, subscriptions) are eligible for refund within 14 days if less than 25% of the content has been consumed.

## How to request
1. Open the **Orders** page from your account dashboard.
2. Select the order and click **Request refund**.
3. Choose a reason and submit. You'll get a confirmation email within minutes.

## Processing time
- Approved refunds appear on your original payment method within **5–10 business days**.
- Bank processing delays may add 1–2 extra days in some regions.

## Exceptions
- Custom-configured hardware is non-refundable once assembly has started.
- Gift cards are non-refundable.
- Items marked "Final Sale" on the product page cannot be returned.
