# Account Help

## Creating an account
Click **Sign up** in the top-right corner, enter your email, and choose a password of at least 8 characters. We'll send a verification link — click it within 24 hours to activate the account.

## Resetting your password
1. Click **Sign in**, then **Forgot password?**
2. Enter the email tied to your account.
3. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a reset link valid for 30 minutes.
4. Choose a new password. Don't reuse passwords from other sites.

## Updating your email or profile
Go to **Account → Profile**. You can change your display name, email, phone, and marketing preferences. Changing your email requires re-verification.

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA. Go to **Account → Security → Two-factor** and choose SMS or an authenticator app (TOTP). Authenticator apps are more secure.

## Deleting your account
You can request account deletion from **Account → Privacy → Delete account**. We'll permanently remove your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g. tax records).
