# Shipping & Delivery

We ship to 40+ countries. Below is everything you need to know.

## Options and costs
| Method        | Estimated time | Cost        |
|---------------|----------------|-------------|
| Standard      | 5–8 business days | Free over $50, otherwise $4.99 |
| Express       | 2–3 business days | $9.99       |
| Overnight     | 1 business day   | $19.99      |

Orders placed before 2pm local time ship the same day. Orders placed after 2pm ship the next business day.

## Tracking
You'll receive a tracking link by email as soon as your order leaves the warehouse. You can also view real-time status on the **Orders** page.

## International duties
For shipments outside the US/EU, the destination country may charge import duties and taxes. These are not included in our shipping fee and are the customer's responsibility.

## Lost or damaged packages
If tracking shows "delivered" but you didn't receive it, wait 48 hours, then contact us. We'll open a carrier investigation and reship if not located within 5 business days.
