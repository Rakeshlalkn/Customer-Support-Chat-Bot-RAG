# Billing & Subscriptions

## Plans
- **Free** — Up to 3 projects, community support.
- **Pro** ($12/month) — Unlimited projects, priority email support, advanced analytics.
- **Team** ($29/user/month) — Everything in Pro, plus SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated success manager.

## Updating payment method
Go to **Account → Billing → Payment methods**. You can add, remove, or set a default card. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

## Invoices
Every paid charge generates a PDF invoice emailed to the billing contact. Past invoices are downloadable from **Account → Billing → Invoices** for the last 7 years.

## Cancelling a subscription
You can cancel anytime from **Account → Billing → Subscription**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you keep access until then. We don't charge cancellation fees.

## Failed payments
If a charge fails, we retry up to 3 times over 7 days and email you each time. If all retries fail, the account downgrades to Free at the end of the grace period. No data is deleted.
